Frequently asked questions

Before your boutique visit.

Do I need an appointment for measurements?

Appointments are recommended so the team can reserve time for fabric review, styling advice, and detailed measurements.

Can I discuss a design on WhatsApp first?

Yes. Share reference images, preferred colors, measurements, and occasion date on WhatsApp for a quick first consultation.

Do you offer tailoring courses?

Yes. RD Advance Boutique offers boutique-focused stitching and tailoring training from basics to advanced designer techniques.

What should I bring for a custom outfit consultation?

Bring your fabric if available, reference photos, matching accessories, and any fitting preferences for the final silhouette.
